Lab Website

Welcome to the Sensing, Embedded, Networking, and Software Engineering Lab at BITS Pilani Goa Campus. The group primarily focuses on designing and building sensing systems using both off-the-shelf, as well as custom mobile and wearable devices. The sensing systems focus on understanding human and agent activities and behaviors in space of interest.
